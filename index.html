Hardware Consultancy

Different types of applications put different computational loads on your system. Allocating your budget in accordance with the workload is very important and could mean the difference between enjoying your purchase and buyer's remorse.

PC Building

I've been building computers for 4 years now, for myself and others, and the passion has only gotten stronger. There are few things as satisfying as slotting in a new GPU. One of them, though, is someone using something you made for them day in and day out. Which is why I love it all the more.

Troubleshooting

Traditional puzzles never really interested me. A malfunctioning computer though is a whole other ball game. Be it incorrectly seated RAM, a faulty part, or some sort of malware, the root cause, learning and resolving it has it's own charm.
